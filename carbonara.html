Pasta Carbonara

Description

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Bacon
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a pan.
  2. Add bacon and chopped garlic. Fry until crispy.
  3. In a separate pot, boil spaghetti to al dente.
  4. Drain spaghetti and add to the bacon pan. Remove pan from stove.
  5. Stir in black pepper and parmesan while hot.
  6. Add grated parmesan on top.
